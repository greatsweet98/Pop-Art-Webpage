How does and will design evolve throughout the years?

The different trends and concepts of design on daily things and objects traditionally and digitally vary depending on the mass media's preference of themes, norms, & approaches within a certain period in the world of 'popular culture'. Ever since the past decades like the 1930s and the 1940s popularizing their own designs of "pop culture trends" on print media like billboards, newspapers, & posters, they evolve and adapt at a certain time period of newer and later trends throughout the years, until we've come to a current age where many typical designs both on print media and in digital media are more contemporary, modern, & minimal, even though people caught up with the trends try to be as artistic as possible.
